Rules

Matching games are games that require players to match similar elements. Participants need to find a match for a picture, or card. For example,composed of 15 pairs, face down in random order. A Match: A player makes a match if the two cards turned picture-side-up are identical. A Miss: A player misses if the two cards turned over are not identical. When a player misses, he or she turns the two cards picture-side-down again, in the same place.
